GoExport Chrome Extension Settings

These settings integrate into GoExport. Make sure you have it installed and added to your path.

The aspect ratio determines how wide your video should be. Default is 16:9 (widescreen).

The resolution is based on aspect ratio and determines how big the frame of the video should be. Default is 720p.

Automatically open the destination folder after GoExport completes.

GoExport will add an outro to exported videos when enabled.

Ensures that OBS is running before starting the export process.
